Event Description

Dive deep into the world of Generative AI! This 24-hour hackathon challenges you to build innovative applications using the latest in large language models, diffusion models, and other generative technologies.

Whether you're a coder, a designer, or just an idea person, team up to create something amazing. We'll provide access to cutting-edge APIs, datasets, and mentors from the industry to help bring your vision to life.

What to Expect:

  • 24 hours of focused building and collaboration.
  • Expert mentors from top AI companies.
  • Workshops on API integration and prompt engineering.
  • Amazing prizes for top teams in categories like "Most Innovative," "Best Technical Implementation," and "Best User Experience."
