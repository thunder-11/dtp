Tech Titans

The official technology and coding club of the campus. We are a community of thinkers, creators, and innovators passionate about leveraging technology to solve real-world problems.

Founded in 2015, Tech Titans has grown from a small group of coding enthusiasts into the largest and most active technical club on campus. Our mission is three-fold: to foster a culture of collaborative learning, to provide a platform for students to work on real-world projects, and to bridge the gap between academia and industry.

We believe in learning by doing. Through our regular workshops, hackathons, and long-term projects, members get hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies. Our vibrant community supports members at all skill levels, from absolute beginners taking their first steps in coding to seasoned developers building complex applications.
